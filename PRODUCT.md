# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Teachers, school administrators, and potentially parents/students. They use this in a school or office environment, likely on both desktop (for heavy data entry/analysis) and mobile (for quick checks in the classroom).

## Product Purpose
A comprehensive student care and management dashboard (SMART STUDENT CARE / SCIDAS). It allows teachers to track academic performance, attendance, behavioral records, and conduct risk analysis or home visits to provide better support for individual students.

## Brand Personality
Professional, reliable, empathetic, clear, and modern. It should feel like a supportive tool that reduces cognitive load for teachers, not another bureaucratic chore.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, dense legacy government or school administration systems.
- Overly playful or childish themes (this is a tool for professionals).
- Grey, sterile "enterprise" dashboards with no visual hierarchy.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over density:** Give data room to breathe. Highlight what needs attention (e.g., at-risk students) rather than overwhelming with all data at once.
- **Empathetic workflows:** Actions should feel supportive and focused on student care.
- **Responsive reliability:** The mobile experience must be a first-class citizen (PWA) so teachers can use it on the go.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Clear contrast for text (WCAG AA).
- Color should not be the only indicator of status (e.g., use icons + colors for at-risk flags).
- Easy to tap targets on mobile devices.
